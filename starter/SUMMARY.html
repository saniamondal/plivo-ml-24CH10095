🔬 Feature Groups (42 total)

⚡ Energy (6) Trailing RMS means (150ms, 500ms), energy slopes at 300ms and 150ms windows, variance, relative tail drop-off.
🎵 F0 / Pitch (8) Terminal slopes (5-frame, 10-frame), final 3-frame normalised ratio, voiced fraction, F0 range, CV, trailing mean.
📏 Syllable Lengthening (2) Last voiced-stretch duration; ratio of final to mean stretch (final-syllable lengthening cue).
📈 Spectral (5) Centroid (mean+slope), rolloff, spectral flux, bandwidth — all at tail of segment.
🔤 MFCCs + Δ (10) MFCCs 1–5 trailing means; MFCC deltas 1–5 trailing means (phonetic tail character).
🗣️ Speaking Rate (2) Voiced-stretch count per second; mean voiced-stretch duration — language-independent syllable-rate proxy.
💬 Discourse Context (5) Pause index, pause_start time, cumulative prior silence, cumulative speech, speech/silence ratio.
📉 Full-turn Energy (4) Turn-level trailing energy minus mean, turn-level energy slope, first-to-last energy change, Q25 tail shift.

🔧 Key Design Decisions

  • Causal   Audio strictly up to pause_start. Zero future leakage.
  • Relative   All pitch/energy features normalised relative to speaker mean — cross-lingual robust.
  • Metric   Optimised for latency@≤5% cutoff, not accuracy.
  • Validation   GroupKFold(5) by turn_id — no pause-level leakage.
  • Calibration   Isotonic calibration aligns p_eot to true EOT probability.
  • Training   Joint EN+HI training (496 pauses) improves Hindi generalisation.

💡 Human vs AI Contributions

👤 Human (Lead Architect & Strategist)

  • Crucial Validation Fix: Identified flaw in initial random splitting and mandated GroupKFold by turn_id, revealing the true signal.
  • Cross-Lingual Insight: Caught the AI using absolute F0 and forced a switch to normalised relative trajectories, making Hindi transfer possible.
  • Linguistic Intuition: Dictated the core features (energy decay, final-syllable lengthening) based on phonetic theory.
  • Leakage Prevention: Caught a cumulative silence bug that used future data, and mandated the strict 5-step leakage audit.
  • Model & Data Strategy: Chose GBT for non-linear prosodic boundaries and decided to train jointly on English + Hindi.
